Our Simulations

IntentSim employs multi-layered agent-based simulations to model proto-universes governed by intent.

Key Simulation Components

  • Adaptive Particles: Learning agents that evolve through interactions.
  • Intent Fields: Probabilistic fields that guide particle behavior.
  • Simulation Modes:
    • Baseline: Random interactions.
    • Adaptive Probabilistic: Intent-driven actions.
    • Energy Conservation: Thermodynamic constraints.
    • Full Features: Combined mechanisms.

Research Findings

Our simulations reveal:

  • Spontaneous complexity growth.
  • Emergent clustering and structure formation.
  • Phase transitions and emergent behavior.
  • Information-driven dynamics.
