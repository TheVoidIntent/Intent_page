IntentSim: Exploring the Information-Intent Nexus

Welcome to IntentSim, an experimental platform exploring the groundbreaking concept of the Information-Intent Nexus. Our research delves into the possibility that the universe's evolution is driven not solely by physical laws, but by intent acting upon information.

Key Points:

  • IntentSim is a live simulation environment.
  • We investigate how intent-driven information filtering can lead to emergent complexity.
  • This project challenges the conventional view of a purely deterministic universe.
